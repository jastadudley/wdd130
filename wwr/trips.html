Trips for all skill levels—and ages!

Whether you're a seasoned pro or never stepped foot in a raft, we have a trip for you. With our menu of selections there is something for everyone!

1-2 Days | Intro Trip: Survival Basics by the Riverside

  • Learn the basics of a raft and emergency procedure.
  • Learn beginner survival skills such as making a fire.
  • Gain confidence on the water with the help of our safe and prepared crew.

7-11 Days | Intermediate Trip: Adventure and Exploration

  • Navigate rapids and rugged terrain.
  • Learn intermediate survival techniques.
  • Prepare meals with foraged ingredients.

20+ Days | Advanced Trip: The Ultimate Wilderness Challenge

  • Minimal gear: hunt, fish, and forage.
  • Face rainstorms and cold nights.
  • Build rafts and track wildlife.
